Chese Sandwiches can be grilled or fried so that the bread toast and the cheese melts(a dish refered to as a grilled cheese sandwhich, or, simply grilled cheese) A grilled cheese is often heated by placing the buttered slices of bread with the cheese between the slices, on a frying pan or griddle. Another variation of grilled cheese sandwhich is to melt and fry the sheese on the outside of the beard as well as melting cheese inside the two slices of bread. When this is done,it produces a crunch fried cheese exterior to the sandwhich.
